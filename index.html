About

"Everything you need is already within you." — Hermann Hesse, Siddhartha

I grew up in Sarlahi, Nepal — curious enough to try making soap out of foam and getting scolded by my mom for it. That curiosity led me to physics, and physics led me to labs, instruments, and a deep belief that science should serve people.

I am currently a third-year Physics student at SRM University, Andhra Pradesh, on an 80% merit scholarship. My work sits at the intersection of experimental condensed matter physics and quantum technology. I do not just study things — I build and measure them.

Outside the lab I read philosophy — Siddhartha, The Alchemist, The Untethered Soul, The Peaceful Warrior. I believe the best engineers are also the most reflective ones.
